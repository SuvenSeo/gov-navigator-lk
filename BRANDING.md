# GovCompanion Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name**: GovCompanion  
**Tagline**: Government Services Made Simple  
**Subtitle**: Your Trusted Government Companion  
**Mission**: Empower Sri Lankan citizens to navigate government services with clarity, confidence, and trust.

---

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Core Characteristics
- **Warm & Approachable**: Friendly without being casual. We're here to help.
- **Clear & Direct**: No jargon. Simple language that everyone understands.
- **Trustworthy**: We're reliable, accurate, and official sources backed.
- **Empowering**: We help citizens take action, not overwhelm them.
- **Local & Proud**: Rooted in Sri Lankan identity and culture.

### Voice Guidelines
- Use "you" and "we" — create conversation, not distance
- Avoid bureaucratic language (never "aforementioned", "pursuant to")
- Be encouraging when users feel lost or overwhelmed
- Acknowledge challenges but focus on solutions
- Celebrate small wins ("You're almost there!")

---

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Maroon** (#6B1420): Sri Lankan heritage, authority, stability
  - Dark variant: #3D0A11
  - Light variant: #8B2340
- **Gold** (#D4A017): Aspiration, trust, premium quality
  - Light variant: #E8C644
  - Dark variant: #C29200

#### Secondary Colors
- **Cream** (#F5EBE0): Warmth, accessibility, clarity
- **Night** (#0F0A0C): Dark mode elegance
- **Emerald** (#27AE60): Growth, progress, successful actions
- **Sand** (#D4B5A0): Softness, balance

#### Usage
- Maroon = Main hierarchy, primary actions, authority
- Gold = Accents, highlights, call-to-action buttons, premium elements
- Emerald = Success states, confirmations, positive feedback
- Cream/Sand = Backgrounds, reduced opacity for secondary elements

### Typography

- **Primary Font**: System default or Inter (modern, clean)
- **Sinhala Font**: Custom serif for cultural authenticity
- **Hierarchy**:
  - H1: 24–32px, Black (900), tracking-tight
  - H2: 18–24px, Black (900)
  - Body: 14–16px, Regular (400)
  - Small: 10–12px, Medium (500)

### Logo & Icon
- **Lion Motif**: Central to brand identity (SVG, animated, responsive)
  - Gold primary, maroon secondary variant
  - Represents strength, local pride, protection
  - Used at various scales (44px hero, 36px header, 18px inline)

### Design Language
- **Borders**: Soft, 1–2px, often with opacity for depth
- **Shadows**: Premium, glow effects (e.g., `shadow-lk-glow`)
- **Radius**: Medium roundness (12–16px for containers, full for pills)
- **Animation**: Smooth transitions (200–400ms), easing functions like cubic-bezier(0.16,1,0.3,1)

## Naming Conventions

### Feature Names
- Service = Government process or document (NIC application, passport)
- Query = User question or request
- Guide = Step-by-step instructions
- Companion = The product (always capitalized)

### URL/Domain
- Primary: `govcompanion.lk` (ideal)
- Alternative: `hello.govcompanion.lk`
- Social: `@govcompanion_lk`

---

## Usage Across Channels

### Website
- Hero: Bold, welcoming, confidence-building
- Navigation: Clear pathways to services
- Footer: Official links (GIC 1919, immigration.gov.lk, drp.gov.lk)

### Social Media
- Tone: Slightly warmer, more conversational
- Content: Tips, success stories, service spotlights
- Hashtags: #GovCompanion #SriLankaServices #SimplifyGov

### Help/Support
- Patient, encouraging tone
- Answer like a trusted friend who knows government
- Celebrate when users figure things out

### Documentation/Legal
- Formal but still approachable
- Privacy: "We protect your information like we protect ours"
- Terms: Clear, no surprises

---

## What We Don't Do

❌ Use government jargon without explanation  
❌ Make promises we can't keep (we guide, don't guarantee outcomes)  
❌ Ignore accessibility (color contrast, keyboard nav, alt text)  
❌ Forget that users are often frustrated or stressed  
❌ Position ourselves as government (we're a third-party guide)

## Brand Personality Chart

| Attribute | Scale | Example |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| Formal ↔ Casual | 60% formal, 40% warm | "Here's your NIC guide (not: yo bro, lemme help) |
| Corporate ↔ Human | 70% human, 30% corporate | We say "we help" not "our solution enables" |
| Technical ↔ Simple | 80% simple, 20% technical | Avoid jargon; explain when needed |
| Local ↔ Global | 85% local, 15% global | Sri Lankan focus; mention diaspora when relevant |

## Metrics to Protect Brand

- **User Sentiment**: 4.5+ stars (trust is core)
- **Accuracy Rate**: 99%+ (verified sources only)
- **Response Time**: <2 seconds (reliability)
- **Satisfaction**: "Would recommend" >85%

## Questions? Apply These Filters

1. **Does it simplify government for users?** → Keep it
2. **Does it build trust?** → Keep it
3. **Is it clear and clear to a 70-year-old?** → Keep it
4. **Does it feel warm but professional?** → Keep it
5. **Is it rooted in our Sri Lankan identity?** → Preferred
